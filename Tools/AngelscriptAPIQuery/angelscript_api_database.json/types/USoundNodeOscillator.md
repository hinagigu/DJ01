# USoundNodeOscillator

**继承自**: `USoundNode`

Defines how a sound oscillates

## 属性

### AmplitudeMin
- **类型**: `float32`
- **描述**: An amplitude of 0.25 would oscillate between 0.75 and 1.25.

### AmplitudeMax
- **类型**: `float32`
- **描述**: An amplitude of 0.25 would oscillate between 0.75 and 1.25.

### FrequencyMin
- **类型**: `float32`
- **描述**: A frequency of 20 would oscillate at 10Hz.

### FrequencyMax
- **类型**: `float32`
- **描述**: A frequency of 20 would oscillate at 10Hz.

### OffsetMin
- **类型**: `float32`
- **描述**: Offset into the sine wave. Value modded by 2 * PI.

### OffsetMax
- **类型**: `float32`
- **描述**: Offset into the sine wave. Value modded by 2 * PI.

### CenterMin
- **类型**: `float32`
- **描述**: A center of 0.5 would oscillate around 0.5.

### CenterMax
- **类型**: `float32`
- **描述**: A center of 0.5 would oscillate around 0.5.

### bModulateVolume
- **类型**: `bool`

### bModulatePitch
- **类型**: `bool`
