# FAddMaterialToCollectionDataflowNode

Adds Outside/Inside Materials to Outside/Inside faces

## 属性

### OutsideMaterial
- **类型**: `UMaterial`
- **描述**: Outside material to assign to the outside faces from the face selection

### InsideMaterial
- **类型**: `UMaterial`
- **描述**: Inside material to assign to the inside faces from the face selection

### bAssignOutsideMaterial
- **类型**: `bool`
- **描述**: If true, the outside material will be assigned to the outside faces from the face selection

### bAssignInsideMaterial
- **类型**: `bool`
- **描述**: If true, the inside material will be assigned to the inside faces from the face selection

### bActive
- **类型**: `bool`
